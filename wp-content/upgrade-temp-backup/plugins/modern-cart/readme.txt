== Description ==

Modern Cart is the ultimate WooCommerce side cart plugin that replaces your clunky default cart page with a smooth slide-out cart drawer or popup cart – all powered by AJAX so customers never leave the page they're on.

Stop losing sales to the default WooCommerce cart page. Every time a shopper gets redirected to a separate cart page, you lose momentum. A WooCommerce side cart fixes this instantly so the cart slides open right on the page, no redirect, no reload, no lost sale.

Add a free shipping progress bar, in-cart upsells, and a built-in coupon form on top, and every cart visit becomes an opportunity to grow your average order value.

== Why Your Store Needs a WooCommerce Side Cart ==

The default WooCommerce cart page kills conversions. Shoppers click "Add to Cart," get bounced to a separate page, and lose interest. That friction costs you real money every single day.

A WooCommerce side cart (also called a cart drawer, sliding cart, or floating cart) solves this immediately. The cart opens right there on the page – no redirect, no reload.

A floating cart icon stays pinned in the corner at all times so shoppers always know what's in their cart and can open it from anywhere on your store.

== Powerful Features 🚀 ==

* **WooCommerce Side Cart / Slide-Out Cart Drawer** – A smooth slide-in panel opens from the side of the screen when a product is added or the floating cart icon is clicked. Customers view, update, and manage their entire cart without leaving the current page.
* **Popup Cart** – Prefer a popup over a slide-out? Switch to popup cart mode for a centered overlay experience. Choose the style that fits your store – both are included.
* **Floating Cart Icon** – A sticky floating cart icon stays pinned in the corner of every page, showing a live item count badge. One click opens the full WooCommerce side cart from anywhere on your site.
* **Free Shipping Progress Bar** – Show customers exactly how close they are to free shipping with a dynamic progress bar inside the cart drawer. The single most effective way to increase average order value – customers consistently add one more item just to unlock it.
* **Cart Rewards Progress Bar** – Go beyond free shipping. Set custom reward milestones – free gifts, discounts, or any incentive – with a real-time progress bar that updates as items are added or removed.
* **Built-In Coupon Form** – Let customers apply discount codes right inside the WooCommerce side cart. No page reloads, no confusion. Works seamlessly with Power Coupons for WooCommerce for auto-apply and rule-based discounts.
* **In-Cart Upsells and Cross-Sells** (Pro) – Recommend relevant products directly inside the sliding cart at the exact moment customers are closest to checking out. The perfect trigger for increasing order value.
* **AJAX Cart – No Page Reloads** – Adding, removing, and updating quantities all happen instantly via AJAX. No page refresh ever. The floating cart icon and side cart always show live, up-to-date totals.
* **Customization and Design Flexibility** – Choose from multiple cart styles and customize colors, button labels, coupon placeholder text, icon styles, and shipping bar messages to match your store's branding perfectly.
* **Mobile-First Design** – The WooCommerce side cart and floating cart icon are built mobile-first. The sliding cart and cart drawer work beautifully on all screen sizes – especially phones and tablets where cart abandonment is highest.
* **Lightweight and Fast** – Built for performance. Modern Cart won't slow your WooCommerce store down.
* **GDPR Compliant** – Modern Cart does not collect or store any personal data from shoppers and is fully GDPR compliant.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is the difference between a side cart, cart drawer, and floating cart? =

They all refer to the same type of WooCommerce cart experience. A WooCommerce side cart (or cart drawer, or sliding cart) is a panel that slides in from the side of the screen when a product is added or the cart icon is clicked. A floating cart refers to the sticky cart icon always visible in the corner of the page. Modern Cart includes all of these in one plugin.

= Does Modern Cart work as a WooCommerce cart drawer? =

Yes! The slide-out side cart is a full WooCommerce cart drawer. It slides in from the side when a product is added or the floating cart icon is clicked. Customers can manage their entire cart without leaving the page – no redirect, no reload.

= Can I use this as a WooCommerce mini cart? =

Absolutely. Modern Cart works as a complete WooCommerce mini cart replacement. The floating cart icon shows the live item count at all times, and clicking it opens the full side cart drawer or popup. No separate mini cart widget is needed.

= How does the free shipping bar work inside the WooCommerce cart? =

Enable the free shipping progress bar in Modern Cart settings. It automatically reads your WooCommerce free shipping threshold and displays a dynamic progress bar inside the side cart showing customers exactly how much more they need to spend to qualify. It consistently drives customers to add one more item – one of the most effective AOV tactics available.

= How does the AJAX cart work in the WooCommerce side cart? =

Every cart action – adding products, updating quantities, removing items, applying coupons – happens via AJAX with zero page reload. The floating cart icon and side cart drawer always show live, up-to-date totals instantly as customers shop.

= Can customers apply coupons inside the WooCommerce side cart? =

Yes! The built-in coupon form lets customers enter and apply discount codes directly inside the cart drawer. Totals update instantly. For rule-based, auto-apply, and bulk coupons, pair it with Power Coupons for WooCommerce.

= Does Modern Cart support WooCommerce in-cart upsells? =

In-cart upsells and cross-sells are available in Modern Cart Pro. Product recommendations appear directly inside the sliding cart or popup cart right when customers are closest to checking out – the highest-converting moment to increase order value.

= Does Modern Cart work with CartFlows checkout funnels? =

Yes! Modern Cart and CartFlows are built by the same team (Brainstorm Force) and are designed to work together seamlessly. Use the Modern Cart side cart for a smooth browsing and cart experience, then hand customers off to a CartFlows-optimized checkout for maximum conversions.

= Does Modern Cart replace the WooCommerce cart page? =

Modern Cart adds a side cart drawer and popup cart so shoppers can manage their cart from any page without ever visiting the cart page. The default WooCommerce cart page stays in place for compatibility, but most shoppers will never need to go there.

= Does Modern Cart work with variable products and WooCommerce Subscriptions? =

Yes! Modern Cart supports all WooCommerce product types – simple, variable, grouped, and subscription products. Variable product attributes and subscription details display correctly inside the side cart.

= How does Modern Cart reduce WooCommerce cart abandonment? =

The floating cart icon keeps the cart visible on every page so customers never lose track of their items. The free shipping progress bar motivates customers to add more to unlock rewards. For shoppers who still leave before purchasing, pair Modern Cart with Cart Abandonment Recovery for WooCommerce to send automated recovery emails.

= Can I use Modern Cart on client websites? =

Yes! You can use Modern Cart on your own store and on client websites. Modern Cart Pro supports multiple websites – check the pricing page for site license details: https://cartflows.com/modern-cart-pricing/

= Will Modern Cart slow down my WooCommerce store? =

No. Modern Cart is engineered for performance. It's lightweight and fully optimized – it won't add meaningful overhead to your store's page load time.

= Does it work with my WooCommerce theme and page builder? =

Yes! Modern Cart works with virtually all WooCommerce-compatible themes – Astra, Storefront, GeneratePress, OceanWP, Blocksy, Kadence, Flatsome, Hello Elementor, Neve, and many more. It's also compatible with Elementor, Spectra, Gutenberg, Beaver Builder, Bricks Builder, Oxygen Builder, and Brizy Builder. If you hit a conflict, our support team will sort it out fast.

== Installation ==

1. Install the `Modern Cart for WooCommerce` either via the WordPress plugin directory or by uploading `modern-cart.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to WooCommerce > Modern Cart to configure the settings
4. Make sure to disable caching on your checkout and thank you steps
